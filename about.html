About

Hi, I'm Jen.

I've lived a lot of lives: U.S. servicemember, FBI analyst, national security advisor, corporate leader, wife, mom, and now certified life coach.

At times, from the outside, it all looked solid. Predetermined. On the inside? I was running on fumes and quietly wondering, Is this really it?

Here's what I know for sure: burnout and misalignment doesn't care how impressive your resume is. Reinvention isn't about throwing everything away — it's about reconnecting with yourself so the life you're building actually feels like yours and for you.

My coaching style is equal parts strategy, compassion, and straight talk. I'll hold space for the hard stuff, but I'll also ask the questions that may make you say, "Dang, I hadn't thought of it that way." And if you need someone to gently (or not-so-gently) call you out when you're getting in your own way — I'm your person.

When I'm not coaching, I'm usually by the ocean, trying to figure out what kind of bird that is, or making a cup of tea. Life keeps teaching me that growth doesn't always feel super graceful — and sometimes it looks like a messy middle, a nervous laugh, or a do-over. And honestly? That's where the magic happens.
