Rise & Realign

A transformative coaching partnership to rebuild direction, self-trust, and real momentum.

Is this you?

You've been doing all the things — checking boxes, hitting milestones, keeping it together — and yet something feels fundamentally off. Maybe you've lost the thread of who you actually are under all the roles and responsibilities. Maybe you know what you want to leave behind but have no idea what you're moving toward. Maybe you're just tired of waiting for your life to feel like yours.

Rise & Realign is for the person who's ready to do the deeper work. Not just get unstuck — but actually build something different. Something that fits.

What three months together looks like

We start with a deep-dive conversation to understand where you are, what's worked, what hasn't, and what you actually want your life to look like. From there, we meet every two weeks — six sessions total — with real support between calls so you're never left figuring it out alone.

Each session is a mix of coaching, reflection, and strategy. We'll challenge assumptions, identify patterns, and build real tools you can use. By the end, you'll have clarity on your direction, a stronger relationship with your own instincts, and genuine momentum — not just a plan that sits in a notebook.

What makes this different

This isn't therapy, and it isn't consulting. It's a partnership. Jen brings the questions, the framework, and the accountability. You bring the courage to answer honestly. Together, that combination creates the kind of shifts that actually stick.

The between-session support matters too — because life doesn't pause between coaching calls. When something comes up, you'll have somewhere to turn.

Who it's for

  • You're ready for real change, not just surface-level tweaks
  • You're navigating a significant life or career transition
  • You feel disconnected from your sense of purpose or identity
  • You want a thought partner who's fully invested in your growth
  • You're done waiting for things to feel right on their own
