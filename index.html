About Jen

Hi, I'm Jen.

I've lived a lot of lives: U.S. servicemember, FBI analyst, national security advisor, corporate leader, wife, mom, and now certified life coach. At times, from the outside, it all looked solid. On the inside? I was running on fumes and quietly wondering, Is this really it?

My coaching style is equal parts strategy, compassion, and straight talk. I'll hold space for the hard stuff, but I'll also ask the questions that may make you say, "Dang, I hadn't thought of it that way."
