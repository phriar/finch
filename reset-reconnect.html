Reset & Reconnect

A small, supportive group for humans navigating transition — together.

Is this you?

You're in a season of change — and change is hard, even when it's the right kind. Maybe you've recently left a job, ended a relationship, moved somewhere new, or stepped back from something that used to define you. Maybe you're mid-transition and can't quite see the other side yet. You don't feel broken, but you do feel a little unmoored.

What helps in these moments isn't always one-on-one advice. Sometimes it's being in a room — even a virtual one — with other people who get it. People who are doing the same messy, brave work of figuring out who they are and where they're going.

That's what Reset & Reconnect is built for.

How it works

Over four weeks, you'll meet with a small group of 8 or fewer for a 90-minute live session each week. Each session has a theme — identity, values, fear, forward motion — and combines Jen's guided coaching with reflective exercises and real conversation between participants.

Between sessions, you'll have journal prompts and light accountability to keep the momentum going. This isn't homework — it's just permission to keep thinking, keep noticing, keep moving.

The group size is intentionally small so every person gets to show up fully, not just observe. This is a participation-first experience.

Why group coaching?

There's something that happens in a well-held group that doesn't happen in one-on-one sessions. You realize your experience isn't as unique or shameful as you thought. You get perspective from people outside your usual circle. You witness someone else's breakthrough and it unlocks something in you. Community is part of the medicine here.

Who it's for

  • You're navigating a life transition and want support beyond your inner circle
  • You're looking for community as much as coaching
  • You want a structured experience but not a huge commitment
  • You're curious about coaching and want to try it in a group setting first
  • You thrive when you can learn alongside other people
